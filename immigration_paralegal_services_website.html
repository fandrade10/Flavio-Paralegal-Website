Immigration Services

Professional Paralegal Document Preparation & Filing Support

Navigating the U.S. immigration system can feel overwhelming — but you don’t have to do it alone. We provide reliable, detail-driven immigration document preparation services, helping individuals, families, workers, and organizations file their applications accurately, efficiently, and with confidence.

Important Notice:

All services are limited to document preparation and administrative support. No legal advice is provided. Services are offered under attorney supervision or as permitted by law.

👨‍👩‍👧 Family-Based Immigration Support

Reuniting families is at the heart of immigration. We help you prepare and organize every form and document needed for family petitions and green cards.

We assist with:

  • Family petitions for spouses, parents, and children
  • Adjustment of Status and consular processing
  • Affidavits of Support and financial documentation
  • Fiancé(e) visas and follow-to-join cases
  • Work and travel permits while cases are pending

✔ Accurate forms
✔ Organized supporting evidence
✔ Timely filing with USCIS or U.S. consulates

💼 Employment & Work Visa Preparation

Whether you are an employer, professional, or skilled worker, we help prepare employment-based immigration filings with precision and care.

Including support for:

  • H-1B, H-2A, H-2B work visas
  • L-1 intracompany transfers
  • O and P visas for extraordinary ability, athletes, and entertainers
  • TN professional visas
  • E-1 and E-2 treaty trader and investor visas

We ensure your application is complete, well-documented, and submission-ready.

✝️ Religious Worker Immigration Services

We proudly support religious organizations and workers with immigration document preparation tailored to faith-based service.

Religious immigration support includes:

  • R-1 religious worker visas
  • Special Immigrant Religious Worker petitions (EB-4)
  • Green card applications for qualifying religious workers
  • Church and organization documentation preparation

Our approach respects both the legal requirements and the mission of your organization.

🟢 Employment-Based Green Card Support

Permanent residency is a major milestone. We help prepare employment-based green card filings from start to finish.

Services include:

  • PERM labor certification support
  • Immigrant worker petitions
  • Adjustment of Status or consular processing
  • Work and travel authorization applications

From EB-1 to EB-5 categories, we help ensure nothing is missed.

🤝 Humanitarian Immigration Assistance

For those seeking safety and protection, we provide compassionate and confidential document preparation support.

We assist with:

  • Asylum and withholding of removal applications
  • Work permits for asylum applicants
  • VAWA self-petitions
  • U visas and T visas
  • Temporary Protected Status (TPS)
  • Special Immigrant Juvenile Status (SIJS)

Your story matters — we help present it clearly and professionally.

⚖️ Immigration Court & Removal Defense Support

(Document Preparation Only)

Facing immigration court can be stressful. We assist with organized, court-ready filings to support your case.

Preparation services include:

  • Immigration court forms and filings
  • Cancellation of removal packets
  • Bond motion documentation
  • Prosecutorial discretion requests
  • Exhibit organization and filing

🇺🇸 U.S. Citizenship & Naturalization Services

Becoming a U.S. citizen is a powerful step forward. We help make the process smoother.

Citizenship support includes:

  • Naturalization applications
  • Citizenship certificates
  • Replacement documents
  • Interview preparation packets

🔄 Waivers, Motions & Case Support

When complications arise, proper documentation is critical.

We assist with:

  • Waivers of inadmissibility
  • Motions to reopen or reconsider
  • Follow-to-join requests
  • Record corrections and replacements
